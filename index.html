About the Centre

The National Science Centre located at Pragati Maidan, Delhi is one of Asia’s largest science museums. It promotes scientific awareness through interactive exhibits, robotics displays, and hands-on experiments. Students can explore future technologies such as artificial intelligence, space science, and biotechnology.

Why Visit?

🤖 Robotics Gallery

See robots in action and understand how robotics technology works.

💻 IT & Communication

Learn about computer networks, digital communication, and modern IT systems.

🧪 Interactive Experiments

Hands-on activities that demonstrate real scientific concepts.

🚀 Space Technology

Discover space exploration and future scientific innovations.
